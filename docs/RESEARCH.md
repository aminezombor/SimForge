## Tool and Dependency Evaluation

For every material candidate, assess:

- suitability for the confirmed outcome;
- license and distribution obligations;
- maintenance health and release cadence;
- compatibility with selected platforms and stack;
- security posture, data handling, and required permissions;
- operational limits, cost, offline behavior, and vendor lock-in;
- credible alternatives and exit strategy.
