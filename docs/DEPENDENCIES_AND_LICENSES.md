## Evaluation Requirements

Before adopting a material open-source, official, hosted, or proprietary tool:

1. Confirm it reduces delivery or maintenance risk for a requirement.
2. Verify the license from an authoritative source and record obligations.
3. Review project maintenance, releases, deprecations, and issue health.
4. Confirm platform, runtime, packaging, and dependency compatibility.
5. Review known vulnerabilities, permissions, data flows, and update strategy.
6. Compare credible alternatives and document the final choice in `DECISIONS.md`.
