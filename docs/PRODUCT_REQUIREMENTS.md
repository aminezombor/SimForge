## Requirement Policy

Requirements describe user-visible outcomes, behavior, constraints, or quality expectations—not technical implementation choices. Assign each confirmed requirement a stable ID (`REQ-001`, `REQ-002`, ...). Do not renumber or silently alter confirmed requirements. Proposed changes require explicit owner approval and a linked entry in `DECISIONS.md`.

Allowed priority labels:

- `HACKATHON-P0`
- `HACKATHON-P1`
- `POST-HACKATHON-V1`
- `V2-ISAAC-SIM`
